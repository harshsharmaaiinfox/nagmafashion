Shop Premium Indian Fashion Online at NAGMA

Welcome to NAGMA — your destination for thoughtfully curated Indian and contemporary fashion. From handwoven sarees and bridal lehengas to everyday kurtis and modern dresses, every piece is selected to bring craftsmanship, comfort and modern elegance to your wardrobe.

Sarees Online

Explore our edit of designer sarees, silk sarees, Banarasi sarees, cotton sarees and georgette sarees handpicked for festivals, weddings and everyday wear. Whether you are looking for a timeless drape for a wedding or a light, breezy cotton saree for daily use, our collection balances heritage weaves with contemporary sensibilities.

Lehengas & Bridal Wear

Discover an exclusive lehenga collection for brides and bridesmaids — from heavily embroidered bridal lehengas and lightweight festive lehengas to modern lehenga sets, sharara sets and gharara sets. Crafted in luxurious silks, organza and net, every piece is designed to make your special occasion unforgettable.

Kurtis, Suits & Salwar Kameez

Find everyday-elegant kurtis, kurta sets, salwar kameez and Anarkali suits in fabrics like cotton, chanderi, muslin and rayon. Perfect for work, family gatherings or festive occasions — our ethnic wear pairs traditional silhouettes with modern prints and breathable comfort.

Western & Fusion Wear

From flowy dresses and chic tops to denim, co-ord sets and indo-western outfits — explore versatile pieces that move seamlessly from office to evening. Each style is selected for its fit, fabric quality and ability to make a statement without effort.

Why Shop With NAGMA

Every garment on NAGMA is sourced from skilled artisans and trusted ateliers across India. We offer free shipping on orders above ₹999, easy 7-day returns, secure payments and dedicated customer support — so that your shopping experience is as elegant as the pieces you take home.
