Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction and a smooth shopping experience at Nagma Fashion.

Order Cancellation

At Nagma Fashion, we aim to process orders as quickly as possible. You can cancel your order under the following conditions:

  • Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement or before they are shipped, whichever is earlier.
  • Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is no longer possible.
  • In case of a successful cancellation, the full amount will be refunded to your original payment method.

Return Eligibility

To ensure a fair process, items must meet the following criteria to be eligible for a return or exchange:

  • The return request must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, and invoices must be intact.

Items must be unused and in original packaging with tags intact for a successful return.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return or exchange due to hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Personal care items and innerwear.
  • Items purchased during clearance sales or "Final Sale" events.
  • Items that show signs of wear or damage not reported at the time of delivery.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, it will undergo a quality check. If approved, the refund is processed as follows:

Prepaid Orders

As mandated by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), all refunds for online payments (Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking) are automatically credited back to the original source of payment. No bank details are collected or required.

  • Refund is initiated within 24–48 hours of approval.
  • Credit reflects in your account within 5–7 working days, depending on your bank.
  • No manual action is needed from you.

Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders

Since COD payments are made in cash, an electronic refund requires your bank details. You may choose one of the following at the time of the return request:

  • Bank Transfer: Refund credited to your saved bank account within 5–7 working days.
  • Store Wallet Credit: Instant credit to your Nagma Fashion wallet for future purchases.

Shipping charges (if any) are non-refundable. We do not store or share your financial data with any third party beyond what is required to process the refund.
