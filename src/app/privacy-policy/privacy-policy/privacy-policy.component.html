Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how Nagma Fashion protects your personal information.

Introduction

At Nagma Fashion, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you interact with Nagma Fashion, we may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number and shipping/billing address

Payment Information

Payment details are processed securely through trusted payment gateways. We do not store your card or banking details.

Technical Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and browsing behavior

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Communicate order updates and provide support responses.
  • Improve our website, products, and overall user experience.
  • Send promotional offers (only if you opt-in).
  • Prevent fraud and ensure overall website security.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Nagma Fashion uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and login sessions.
  • Analyzing website traffic and performance metrics.
  • Personalizing content and product recommendations.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections (SSL).
  • Trusted third-party payment gateways.
  • Limited access to sensitive information within our organization.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your account credentials confidential at all times.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only with:

  • Shipping and logistics partners for order delivery.
  • Payment service providers for secure transactions.
  • Legal authorities if required by law to comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information at any time.
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems.
  • Opt out of marketing communications whenever you choose.
